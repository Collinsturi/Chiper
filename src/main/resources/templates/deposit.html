Transfer Cash from M-Pesa to Bank Account

To transfer cash from your M-Pesa account to your bank account, follow these steps:

  1. Dial *234# on your mobile phone and select "M-Pesa"
  2. Select "Lipa na M-Pesa"
  3. Select "Pay Bill"
  4. Enter the Business Number for your bank.
    • For Chiper Bank, enter 369369
  5. Enter your bank account number as the account number
  6. Enter the amount you want to transfer
  7. Enter your M-Pesa PIN
  8. Confirm the transaction
  9. You will receive a confirmation message from M-Pesa and your bank

If you have any questions or issues with the transfer, please contact your bank's customer support.
